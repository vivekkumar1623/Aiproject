Historical Missions:
Apollo Program: (NASA) - Included missions like Apollo 11 (first lunar landing)
Vostok and Voskhod: (Soviet Union) - Early Soviet manned spaceflights
Gemini Program: (NASA) - Precursor to the Apollo program, testing equipment and procedures
Skylab: (NASA) - An early space station
Space Shuttle Program: (NASA) - Columbia, Challenger, Discovery, Atlantis, Endeavour 

Ongoing and Upcoming Missions:
International Space Station (ISS): (International) - A multi-national space station in orbit
Artemis Program: (NASA) - Missions to the Moon, including Artemis I and Artemis II
James Webb Space Telescope: (NASA) - A powerful space telescope
Perseverance Mars Rover: (NASA) - Mission to Mars
Parker Solar Probe: (NASA) - Mission to the Sun
Europa Clipper: (NASA) - Mission to Jupiter's moon Europa
Chandrayaan-3: (ISRO) - Indian lunar mission
Aditya-L1: (ISRO) - India's first solar mission
BepiColombo: (ESA/JAXA) - Mission to Mercury
Tianwen-1: (CNSA) - Chinese Mars mission
Hayabusa2: (JAXA) - Sample return mission to asteroid Ryugu
Lucy: (NASA) - Mission to Jupiter's Trojan asteroids
DAVINCI: (NASA) - Mission to Venus
Comet Interceptor: (ESA) - Mission to study a comet
